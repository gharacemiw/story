Have you ever had the feeling that
life only happens to other people?

I mean: that feeling
the big moments
a job tucked into the gears of the world
a feverish love
explicit signs from the universe,
contemporary prophecy
money, money!

Listen: I also have no answer.
What I can say is this:
it's 11:39 pm
and I'm at a bar counter
writing this poem.
There's a beer in front of me
more or less like this:

![foam / beer / coaster that says Corona Extra](/images/granville-drawing-1.png)

There are people talking laughing
resting their elbows on tables
There are lights that change color
There's a strange
moth-frog
on the counter
and there's the devil
sitting on a tuft of green grass,
upside down
and two skulls and
the number 818

And it seems like we're all
waiting for our turn.

08/30
*11:47 p.m.*
