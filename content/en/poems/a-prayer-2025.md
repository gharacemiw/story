---
title: "A Prayer (2025)"
date: "2025-11-24"
category: "poems"
---

I don't want a thing—
not a spaceship shaped like your big eyes,
or the sun you swallowed yesterday.

Not walking along the seawall,
not eating fries,
not even a studio of my own,
or passing the French test,
or riding with anadyomene on
white horses through clouds
that scatter like
startled snakes as we pass.
I don't want a car, not even summer.
I don't have them.

My desires are already fulfilled:
a pearl-colored mechanical pencil made in Japan,
a shirt full of holes,
and the ability to lean back, sip my burnt coffee, and
flirt with the world.
