---
title: "Trails That Lead to the Wrong Side of the World"
date: "2026-03-22"

translation_of: "pt-br/poemas/trilhas-que-levam-ao-avesso-do-mundo"

Gold in the clouds,
thunders of lava,
underground storms and
a cavern above the Moon:
seven were the entrance portals,
their knobs gleaming with star silver or spit,
the treasures piled up
on the outside.

Here, flowers bloom from the roots,
the lion tamarins communicate by telepathy.
Inside every seed there is a waterfall
that only lives if it has fish
with dry scales, very dry.


On the wrong side of the world
nothing is straight,
only angles exist.

No one speaks with the tongue,
story precedes history
and when it is told, everyone gathers in a square
waiting eagerly
for its beginning.
