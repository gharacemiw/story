---
title: "Meditation (2025)"
date: "2025-01-01"
category: "poems"
excerpt: "Fragile, anxious creature, / bag of blood, flesh, hormones and stories."
---

Fragile, anxious creature,
bag of blood, flesh, hormones and stories.
Make things easier for yourself—
keep your story straight,
and keep an ending in mind
for most days.

Keep your metrics mediocre,
steady allies of consistency.
Pin a medal on yourself for the bare minimum,
carve a narrow path between the towers,
shelter yourself from Madness and Malice.

Get used to returns, restarts, tides of bad luck.
Grow eyes that can spot the direction of the wind,
and a body strong enough to
let it go.
