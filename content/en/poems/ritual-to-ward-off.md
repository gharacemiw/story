---
title: "Ritual to Ward Off the Counterintuitive Nature of the World (2025)"
date: "2025-01-01"
category: "poems"
excerpt: "Rub your hands / with salt / between them."
translation_of: "pt-br/poemas/ritual-para-afastar"
---

Rub your hands
with salt
between them.
Have a
green T-shirt.
Glance, by chance, at the clock
and catch the same hours and minutes.

Learn French.
Do not sleep for 48 (days).
Find the spinal column
of a cloud and
drink Coke Zero.
