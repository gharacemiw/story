---
title: "Prescription (2023)"
date: "2023-01-01"
category: "poems"
excerpt: "Pay attention to the days / when the future doesn't matter all that much."
translation_of: "pt-br/poemas/meditacao-de-2023"
---

Pay attention to the days
when the future doesn't matter all that much.
Those days are even better
than the days
when the future seems marvelous.

You stop yearning for the world
and begins to yearn
for a song or
the nakedness
of the first words
spoken in the morning.

Since there are no few
sources of your weakness,
be resolved to
handle what you've been given, to
suffer without complaint, to
delight without guilt,
to use opportunities like books
read over and over again.

Go on with your life using
this body,
these talents,
this spirit,
and also
these passions.

There is a virtuous part
within you.
It lives on the underside
of the feeble words
you use.

There is a divine part
within you—
if you hold it with
both hands
and squeeze,
you will see the light
slip between your fingers.
